# Idea OverFlow  
### NirMa HackaMined'26 Project

A platform designed to capture, organize, and transform overflowing ideas into structured and actionable insights.

---

## 📖 Overview

**Idea OverFlow** addresses a common challenge faced by students, innovators, and teams — having many ideas but no structured way to manage or refine them.

The platform allows users to capture ideas, categorize them, analyze their potential, and collaborate effectively. It helps convert chaotic brainstorming into organized innovation.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Preparing documents according to specific academic or institutional standards can be difficult because:

- Users may not know the correct structure of the paper
- Documents may miss required sections or subsections
- Formatting inconsistencies occur frequently
- Manually checking documents against guidelines is time-consuming

These issues often lead to **incorrect submissions or extra time spent fixing formatting problems**.

## 💡 Our Solution

Idea OverFlow provides a system that:

- Compares the uploaded document with a standard reference paper
- Detects structural or formatting differences
- Suggests improvements and corrections
- Allows users to add missing **sections and subsections**
- Helps maintain consistency with the required document structure

This helps users quickly transform their document into a format that aligns with the required academic standard.

## 📌 Features

- **Idea Capture** – Quickly record and store ideas in a centralized system  
- **Idea Organization** – Categorize and structure ideas for easier navigation  
- **Idea Analysis** – Evaluate ideas using analytical insights  
- **Collaboration** – Allow multiple users to contribute and refine ideas  
- **Idea Tracking** – Monitor idea development from concept to project
